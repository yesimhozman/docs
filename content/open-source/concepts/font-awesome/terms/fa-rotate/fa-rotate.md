The **`fa-rotate`** classes are used to rotate an icon by 90, 180, or 270 degrees.

## Example

```html
<i class="fas fa-cut"></i>
<i class="fas fa-cut fa-rotate-90"></i>
<i class="fas fa-cut fa-rotate-180"></i>
<i class="fas fa-cut fa-rotate-270"></i>
```
