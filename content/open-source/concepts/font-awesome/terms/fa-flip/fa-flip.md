The **`fa-flip`** classes are used to flip an icon on the horizontal or vertical axis.

## Example

```html
<i class="fas fa-truck"></i>
<i class="fas fa-truck fa-flip-horizontal"></i>
<i class="fas fa-truck fa-flip-vertical"></i>
```
